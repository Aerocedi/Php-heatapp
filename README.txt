   ###### DESCRIPTION #######
   This is a web application designed to calculate the effectiveness , oulet teperatures
   of fluid when the type of fluid , inlet temperatures, mass flowrates, the overall heat transfer coefficent
  and the type of heat exchanger is given. Selecting from range of fluids(WATER, ENGINE OIL ,GLYCERIN , ETHYL ALCOHOL
  AND AMMONIA WITH CONSTANT SPECIFIC HEAT AT ROOM TEPERATURE).
